  • Faculty of Architecture and Civil Engineering

    • 1. Faculty Introduction
    Faculty of Architecture and Civil Engineering has 4 undergraduate specialties (Civil Engineering, Urban and Rural Planning, Architecture and Landscape Architecture) and 1 laboratory center. There are about 2000 registered regular students studying for bachelor's degrees, and a staff of 69, among whom there are 52 faculty members, 4 professors and 14 associate professors, 16 with doctor degrees, 3 part-time advisers of master. The faculty emphasizes practical teaching and training of the practical ability of teachers. Now, more than 20 members of faculty own 36 different kinds of national vocational qualifications. Teachers undertook all levels of projects include one project of national natural science foundation, 9 provincial and ministerial projects, 35 city projects, more than 100 scientific research projects supported by enterprise.
    The faculty has 1 provincial key disciplines of ‘Provincial Twelfth Five-year Plan’, 1 civil engineering experimental teaching demonstration centre, 1 Jiangsu province urban and rural residential structure assembly technology Engineering Laboratory and 1 Huai’an Key Laboratory of solid waste recycling technology. Laboratory center includes 15 laboratories including CAD centre, structural laboratory and material mechanics laboratory, which covers a total area of 3650m2 and the equipment worth 9.987 million RMB. For a long time, the graduates of the faculty are welcomed by the general employers. The graduates possess strong practical capabilities to undertake the plan and design work of city, architecture and landscape architecture, and civil engineering construction and management.

    2. Undergraduate Programs Introduction
    2.1 Civil Engineering
    The program cultivates the undergraduates equipped with scientific literacy and humanistic accomplishment, obtaining the basic theory and professional knowledge of all the fields of civil engineering such as construction engineering, underground engineering, bridge engineering, geotechnical engineering and road engineering. They have strong innovative spirit, teamwork spirit, practical ability and sense of social responsibility.

    2.2 Architecture
    The students will be equipped with innovative and enterprising spirit as well as the sense of social responsibility, understanding the basic theory and engineering practice ability of architectural design, working on the design, teaching and management of architecture.

    2.3 Urban and Rural Planning
    The program of Urban and Rural Planning will cultivate undergraduates who are equipped with the basic theory and skills, as well as the practical ability, innovative spirit, entrepreneurial ability and social responsibility, in capable of working in the fields of urban and rural planning, architectural design and landscape planning.

    2.4 Landscape Architecture
    Landscape Architecture is a comprehensive course which combines Urban and Rural Planning, Architecture and other courses. It fully makes use of methods of science and art to build environment of neighborhoods, towns and cities. As the integrated discipline of urban planning and architecture, it played a major role in fulfilling the growing national need for well-planned and well-designed urban environments, combining several disciplines such as planning and design, landscape plant, engineering science, environment and ecology, culture and art, landscape resource, geology and sociology.
