  • Faculty of Automation

    • 1. Faculty Introduction
    The Faculty of Automation, dating back to the faculty of Electronic and Electrical Engineering, was established in 2015 after professional optimization and adjustment. There are 3 departments (Electrical Engineering, Automation and Measurement & Control) and 56 faculty members including 4 professors and 12 associate professors in the faculty. Among all faculty members , 19 hold doctors' degrees and 14 are master's tutor, 1 was selected as candidates for Jiangsu provincial "333 Project", 2 was selected as candidates for Jiangsu provincial "Six talent peaks project", 6 were selected as candidates for municipal "533 talents project", 1 was awarded as excellent teacher and 2 school pacesetter of HYIT.
    The faculty has established the district discipline of Control Science and Engineering in the institute, focusing on the application researches in control theory and intelligent system, detection technology and application, pattern recognition and signal processing, and new energy system controlling technology. It has also established scientific research and services organizations such as Jiangsu province instrument industry public service platform for small and medium enterprises, and Huai’an new Automation Technology Application Center, etc. There are 3 undergraduate programs in Electrical Engineering and Automation, Automation, Measurement & Control Technology and Instrument, of which Automation is excellent plan software category experimental major of Jiangsu province, and Electrical Engineering and Automation is the featured specialty under construction at school level. The faculty now has 1335 registered students pursuing bachelor's degrees and 26 registered students pursuing professional master's degrees.

    2. Undergraduate Programs Introduction
    2.1 Electrical Engineering and Automation
    This program aims to train and cultivate be high- class application-type talents in the field of electrical engineering and automation. Graduates should possess wider basic theoretical knowledge and practical abilities in electrical engineering and automation. Besides, they should own the abilities to undertake such jobs as system research, development, running and maintenance in automatic control, information process and computer application fields associated with electric engineering.

    2.2 Automation
    Graduates are trained to be (applied senior engineers or technicians) high technical and applied talents in the field of automation. Graduates should possess wider basic theoretical knowledge and practical abilities in automation. In addition, they should own the abilities to undertake such jobs as design and development of automation equipment, integration, application and maintenance in process control, motion control, test and automation instrument, intelligent system.

    2.3 Measuring & Control Technology and Instrument
    This program aims to train and cultivate applied senior engineers or technicians in the field of measurement and controlling technology. Graduates should possess wider basic theoretical knowledge and practical abilities in measuring and controlling technology and instrument. In addition, they should own the abilities to undertake such jobs as engineering design, system operation and maintenance in engineering testing, process control, intelligent instruments, measurement and control system integration.
