    • 1. Faculty Introduction
    The Faculty consists of 3 departments, namely Economics & Trade, Business Administration, and Finance & Accounting. It enjoys 8 undergraduate programs, that is, international economics & trade, economics & finance, financial mathematics, business management, human resources management, marketing, financial management, and accounting. The business management and the financial management are the Key Specialized Principles of HYIT, and the financial management is the Key Specialized Construction Unit for continuous education of Jiangsu Province. There are 2 provincial practical training platform, that is, Jiangsu Provincial Platform for International Business Talents Training Services, and Jiangsu Provincial Center for Business Talents Practical Education, a library and an experiment center, including 8 laboratories respectively for international trade scenarios simulation, financial simulation, ERP sand table simulation, management outward bound, human resources management, e-commerce, business negotiation scenario simulation, and financial information system. More than 20 practice bases have been established. Currently, there are 100 faculty staff members, including 9 professors, 39 associate professors, and 21% of the staff with Doctor Degrees. There are more than 3000 full-time undergraduate students.

    2. Undergraduate Programs Introduction
    2.1 Business Administration (Business Management, HR Management, Marketing, Financial Management)
    After passing exams in the first two years, students will undertake an optional placement year. Students are qualified to choose the courses that they want to follow. Each course is structured to provide them with professionally relevant skills of management, economics, law, which they will pitch to industrial experts of production management, HR management, marketing analysis and accountancy in companies and governments.

    2.2 Accounting
    Each course is structured to provide students with fundamental theories and practical abilities of accounting. They will pitch to the senior personnel of accounting, auditing and financial management. They are introduced with the fundamental theories and analysis of management, economics and finance. Students will study both the basic knowledge and relevant policies and legislation. They will be provided with the skills in bibliography searching for further research

    2.3 International Economics & Trade
    This program aims to train senior applied personnel in the specialized areas of international trade, business management, and economic analysis, who have the academic foundation of economy and international trade, and the comprehensive and practical capability required by business, finance industry and government. The graduates have basic understanding of the theories and skills of economics and international trade, the current situation and trend of the economy of the world and the major countries and regions, being familiar with the prevailing international trade rules, practices, policies and regulations, with knowledge of modern business information technology, mastering the basic methods of literature retrieval and data inquiry, and having the preliminary ability of scientific research and practice.

    2.4 Economics & Finance
    This program aims to train senior applied personnel in the economic and financial areas of banking, securities, funds, insurance, with the capability of analyzing and solving practical problems. It helps the graduates to have basic theories of economics, management, and finance, being familiar with China’s financial management policies, laws and regulations, the relevant international rules, regulations and practices, understand the theoretical frontier and development trend of financial discipline, and master the basic methods of literature retrieval and data inquiry, and have the preliminary ability of scientific research and practice.

    2.5 Financial Mathematics
    This program aims to train senior applied personnel with innovation, entrepreneurship and social responsibility. Students should master the basic knowledge, methods and skills of financial mathematics and financial engineering, being preliminary trained in scientific research, and can solve practical problems of financial markets investment, financing, and risk control. After graduation, students can work in the areas of management, evaluation of financial markets and finical product development of finance.
