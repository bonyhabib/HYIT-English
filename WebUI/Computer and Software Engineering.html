  • Faculty of Computer and Software Engineering

    • 1. Faculty Introduction
    The Faculty of Computer and Software Engineering currently consists of four undergraduate programs including computer science and technology, software engineering, internet of things engineering, and network engineering. The Faculty has successively set up a series of major fields including information security, network engineering, embedded system software design, and internet of things in the program of computer science and technology, multimedia communication in the program of communication engineering, etc. Cooperative education has been successively conducted on the program of computer science and technology with universities in Australia, Singapore, India, etc, and corporations of Jiangsu Microsoft Technology Center, Danet Era Technology Co., Ltd., and Neusoft Group Enterprise. At present, there are more than 1780 students in the Faculty and a total number of more than 200 students have received national and provincial awards.
    The program of Computer Science and Technology is the only provincial A-class program as well as one of the 5 provincial featured specialties in HYIT. The program of Computer including computer science and technology, software engineering, and internet of things engineering is provincial key program. The program of Computer Science and technology is the national pilot specialties undertaking ‘A Plan for Educating and Training Outstanding Engineers (PETOE)’. The program of Computer Science and Technology and Software Engineering is within the first batch of software experimental specialties of provincial outstanding engineers training specialties. The discipline of Software Engineering is first-level provincial key construction discipline, as well as the first-level key discipline of and the special zone of HYIT. The discipline of computer application technology is a second-level key discipline of HYIT. The discipline of Computer Science and Technology is a first-level key discipline of HYIT.

    2. Undergraduate Programs Introduction
    2.1 Computer Science and Technology
    This program aims to train and cultivate kinds of high-quality engineering talents who have strong innovation capacity to meet the demands of economic and social development. It provides students with the general knowledge of this major such as the principles and methods of mechanical design, the principles and technologies of mechanical manufacturing engineering, the drive and control in mechanical system and basics for computer application, which enable students to acquire basic training of modern mechanical engineer and improve their basic ability of mechanical product design, manufacturing & equipment control and production organization management.

    2.2 Software Engineering
    The program of Software Engineering cultivates students to be application oriented software engineering talents who master the basic knowledge of natural science and humanities, computer science, software engineering, and application knowledge, possess preliminary experience in software development and project organization, consciousness of innovation and entrepreneurship, competition and team spirits, and good foreign language abilities, to adapt themselves to technological progress and social demand changes. The graduates should own the basic theory of computer system and software engineering practice ability, and can be engaged in the design, development, maintenance, and other work of computer software, such as enterprises and institutions and administrative departments.

    2.3 Internet of Things Engineering
    The program of Internet of Things Engineering cultivates students to be high-ranking application oriented talents who master basic knowledge and application abilities in computer science and technology, internet of things engineering, and other related fields, possess consciousness of innovation and entrepreneurship, competition, and team spirits, and can be engaged in technology research, engineering design, product development, operation and maintenance, system management, and other related fields. The graduates should own the basic theory and engineering practice ability of computer, Internet of things, and can be engaged in the organization, design, development, management, and operation of the computer system and the Internet of things.

    2.4 Network Engineering
    The program of Network Engineering cultivates students to be engineering application oriented professional talents who possess good scientific literacy, systematically master basic theories, knowledge, skills, and methods in network hardware, software, and applications, to be engaged in research institutions, enterprises, administrative departments, and other network engineering teaching, development and application units. The graduates should have the basic knowledge of network engineering and engineering practice, and can be engaged in the design, development, management and operation and maintenance of network engineering application system in enterprises and institutions and administrative departments.
