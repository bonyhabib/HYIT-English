  • Faculty of Design and Art

    • 1. Faculty Introduction
    The faculty offers five undergraduate programs in Industrial Design, Product Design, Visual Communication Design, Environment Design, and the Digital Media Art. The Industrial Design serves as the Provincial Key Discipline and the Provincial Excellent Engineer Pilot Project in Jiangsu Province. Depending on the school investment and the state and national projects funds, the faculty has set up an Experimental Center and an Undertaking Basis for college students’ Scientific Innovation. The faculty shares a Provincial Experimental Education Center for Design and Art underdevelopment, a Provincial Demonstration Center of Design and Art, and a Municipal Public Service Center of Huai’an. It holds 7 laboratories covering modelling, pottery, carpentering, digital modelling, and printing and fabricating, digital media and computer aided design, 2 multifunctional exhibitions and 6 drawing rooms, and 18 teacher-student Innovation Studios for business running. It has 30 sets of large-scale instruments and appliances, with a total value of more than RMB 8,000,000, and holds a built-up area of 9,000 ㎡. The faculty has 26 practical bases inside and outside of the institute, equipped with favorable practical teaching atmosphere.

    2. Undergraduate Programs Introduction
    2.1 Digital Media Art
    To cultivate high quality talents of digital media art professional training, the program makes students have a solid knowledge and practical skills in digital media art, which can be engaged in digital image creation, network media design, three animation and virtual reality in the relevant media, network media, film and television culture, advertising design, space display, etc. For graduates, they are supposed to work in the TV media, Internet media, film and television culture, advertising design, space display and other enterprises, engaging in digital video, online media, three-dimensional animation and virtual reality and other aspects of the design, development and management.

    2.2 Industrial Design
    Students mainly study the major basic theory and knowledge of industrial design to obtain the professional skills training and develop the competence as an industrial designer. It aims to cultivate students to be an integrated industrial design talent engaged in industrial product innovative design and related areas such as human-computer interaction design, display design and environment of development, research, planning, education and management in enterprises and institutions, professional design institutions and scientific research units with a solid industrial design basic theory, basic knowledge and application ability. And it also intend to equip students with international vision and the sense of social responsibility, comprehensive way of innovative thinking and team cooperation spirit.

    2.3 Product Design
    This program aims to develop practical specialized talents with innovative entrepreneurial spirit and social responsibility to adapt to the needs of the socialist modernization. To equip students with solid products design based on theoretical knowledge and product modeling ability, with good professional skills and professional quality, so students can work in the professional design department, large and medium-sized enterprises and scientific research institutions the in product innovation, management, scientific research or teaching, and are able to adapt to development of the industry.

    2.4 Visual Communication Design
    This program aims to cultivate the high quality applied talents for visual communication design. Graduates should equip themselves with the basic theories and the professional knowledge of visual communication design, can for professional design field, enterprises and institutions, engaged in visual communication, design, planning, management, etc.

    2.5 Environment design
    This program conducts artistic treatment on the space interface by organizing and enclosing the space. It uses the design language such as natural light, artificial lighting, furniture, layout of accessories and styling, with the configuration of plant flower, water, pieces and sculptures to make the indoor and outside space environment reflect the specific atmosphere and style in order to meet people’s need of using and the Visual aesthetic. It is an applied art for the construction of indoor and outdoor space by the means of art design.
