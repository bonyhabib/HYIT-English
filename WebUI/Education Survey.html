    • Education Levels


    HYIT is committed to outstanding higher learning at the levels of undergraduate, postgraduate, continuing and

    international education. Its undergraduate education was rated as excellent by the Ministry of Education in

    2007. The postgraduate education has been growing smoothly. And the University has been moving forward

    steadily to the level of research-led teaching education.




    Discipline Areas


    HYIT is a multi-disciplinary institute which highlights engineering, growing perceptibly in other disciplines,

    including science, agriculture, humanities, economics and management. Among these fields of study, HYIT has

    al-together 44 fulltime four-year undergraduate programs, 3 postgraduate professional master's programs and

    around 17 continuing education programs. Among the academic majors, there are 2 national major-building

    disciplines, 4 provincial distinctive programs, 2 provincial major-building disciplines, 1 provincial brand program,

    2 provincial key disciplines, 4 provincial key laboratories, 7provincial experimental teaching demonstration

    centers for the fundamental courses.
