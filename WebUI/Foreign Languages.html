  • Faculty of Foreign Languages

    • 1. Faculty Introduction
    The Faculty of Foreign Languages consists of three undergraduate programs that enroll undergraduates including English, Business English and Translation, 1 College English Department, and 4 centers, namely Language and Culture Research Center, Translation Center, Business Practice Center as well as Center for Foreign Language Services, and owns one key support discipline at the school level. The faculty is equipped with 34 modern multimedia classrooms, 1350 fixed seats for the students and 2 FM Broadcasting Stations. The library of the faculty provides wonderful conditions for teachers and students to work and study with a collection of 20, 000 books, more 70 Chinese and foreign journals, and increasingly updated audio and visual materials.
    The faculty attaches great importance to class teaching. Up to now, quite a few teachers have been awarded the First Prize, the Second Prize and the Third Prize of the FLTRP Star Teacher Contest and the SFLEP National Foreign Language Teaching Contest. The passing rate of TEM 4 and TEM８has gradually improved year after year. More than 30 students have furthered their study at home and board by taking the postgraduate entrance examination. The graduation rate maintains over 96% and the graduates are now performing excellently in a variety of fields including foreign trade, finance, translation, press and publication, English education and so on.

    2. Undergraduate Programs Introduction
    2.1 English
    This program involves listening, speaking, reading, writing, and translation, which form an organic whole. Students majoring in English mainly study the basic knowledge of English language and literature, and also the knowledge of humanities and social science including literature, history, philosophy, politics, economy, art, law, and so on of the main English-speaking countries. They accept the systematic and scientific English training of the basic English skills, namely, listening, speaking, reading, writing, and translation, to achieve excellence in expressing themselves both orally and in written form, inter-cultural communication, acquiring knowledge gradually via computer and the internet, and to achieve their abilities in discovering, analyzing, and solving problems, in creative thinking, and scientific research by applying their professional knowledge.

    2.2 Business English
    Business English aims to learn English language, literature, basic economic and trade knowledge and practice, to obtain the basic skills of English language including listening, speaking, reading, writing and translation, and etc., achieve a strong ability to use English and engage in economic and trade practices, and gain access to information and other aspects of practical skills.
    This program cultivates application-type and compound talents who can meet the needs of socialist modernization construction and local economic and social development, who have the all-round development, with the spirit of innovation and entrepreneurship, and the sense of social responsibility, who gain the acquisition of solid foundation of English language, skilled use of English, and understand the society, economy and culture of major English-speaking countries, master the knowledge of international economic trade, and familiarize themselves with foreign trade practice.

    2.3 Translation
    The students majoring in Translation mainly study the basic theory and basic knowledge of language and translation, accept the bilingual training of language knowledge and language skills, master the basic skills of inter-cultural communication and Chinese and foreign translation and interpretation, and master the basic ability of translation and interpretation.
    This program cultivates application-type talents in interpretation and translation， who can meet the needs of socialist modernization construction and local economic and social development, who have the all-round development, a solid foundation of bilingual knowledge, rich encyclopedic knowledge, higher inter-cultural communicative competence, strong logical thinking and good professional ethics, who understand Chinese and foreign social culture, familiarize themselves with translation theories, grasp the translation and interpretation skills of texts in different styles, and can proficiently use translation tools and understand the related professional process.
