  • Faculty of Humanities

    • 1. Faculty Introduction
    Humanities Faculty offers three undergraduate programs，Social Work, Public Affairs Management and Secretary Science. And it has a comprehensive laboratory including Social Work, Public Affairs Management, Secretary Science and other experimental chamber. There are 36 staff members, including 32 full-time teachers, 1 professor, 8 associate professors, 27 holding doctorate and master's degree. There are 650 students. Besides undertaking three professional talent training, the Faculty undertakes the optional courses of humanities and social sciences in the institute. On the basis the two institute-level original key Level II disciplines, sociology and politics theories, the Faculty prepares to built the discipline of public management which is listed as the key construction discipline by the institute. The team has about 50 members.

    2. Undergraduate Programs Introduction
    2.1 Public Affairs Management
    This Program aims to cultivate application-oriented advanced talents. The students can access to public affairs management training. Before graduation, students should master the basic principles and knowledge of Management, Law and Sociology. With the spirit of public service and their innovative ability, students will be engaged in related public affairs management, services and research work for e-government, urban management and public human resource management.

    2.2 Social Work
    This program aims to cultivate senior applied talents with practical and all-round developing capacities that meet the demands of socialist modernization and local social and economic development, have innovative spirit and social responsibility and have a good command of knowledge in sociology, management and psychology domains

    2.3 Secretary Science
    This program aims to cultivate high quality applied talents working in the field of secretarial and management. Graduates are supposed to possess a wide range of basic theoretical knowledge of Secretary Science and strong practical ability what can make them engage in the secretarial science related work such as public relations, human resource management, and administrative assistants.
