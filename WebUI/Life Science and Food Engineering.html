  • Faculty of Life Science and Food Engineering

    • 1. Faculty Introduction
    Faculty of Life Science & Food Engineering, originating from the Life Science and Chemical Engineering Faculty, was set up in June 2015. The Faculty consists of 7 undergraduate degree programs, including biological engineering, food science and engineering, horticulture, agriculture, food quality and safety, landscape, new energy science and engineering, and 1 professional master degree program in biochemical engineering. The school now hosts 1669 undergraduate students, 9 graduate candidates. Currently, there are two fields of Disciplines Conferring Academic degrees of Engineering and Agriculture Science, covering 5 Level I academic disciplines including Biological Engineering, Food Science and Engineering, Agriculture, Horticulture, Landscape.

    2. Undergraduate Programs Introduction
    2.1 Bioengineering
    Bioengineering program is National and Jiangsu Provincial featured specialty as well as one of the provincial key construction disciplines. We have a Biological Engineering Disciplines Integrative Training Center and Jiangsu Provincial Engineering Laboratory for Biomass Conversion and Process Integration. There are 20 full-time faculty members in the department, including 4 professors, 7 associate professors, 10 holding doctor degrees and 9 postgraduate tutors.

    2.2 Agronomy
    Agronomy Program is oriented to train and cultivate practical special talents who are all-round developed, with a solid professional knowledge of agriculture, basic theory and basic skills, innovation and entrepreneurial spirit, the spirit of teamwork, social responsibility. Students are supposed to have the basic concepts, related knowledge and skills of the crop production, crop genetics and breeding, plant protection, seed production etc. Graduates are suppose to be able to do professional jobs associated with agricultural technology research and development, science and technology, production and management, etc.

    2.3 Horticulture
    Horticulture Program is oriented to prepare all-around developed students with solid professional knowledge of gardening, basic theory and skills, as well as with innovation and entrepreneurial spirit, teamwork spirit and social responsibility. The graduates are qualified to engage in work associated with gardening technology research and development, science and technology, production and management, etc. and jobs related to vegetables, flowers, fruit trees, horticulture, ornamental horticulture and other relevant industries.

    2.4 Food Science and Engineering
    This cross-disciplinary program, based on the chemistry, biology and chemical engineering, is to do research on the basic theories and skills associated with the processing, application of modern food science, and engineering. This program is oriented to train and cultivate advanced special engineering talents who acquire basic training of first-tier engineers. The graduates are supposed to be qualified for the jobs such as food production and processing, technology management, quality control, technology development, engineering design, marketing and other relevant work in food production, processing and circulation enterprises, food and agricultural products inspection agencies, supervision and management departments, scientific research institutes and other relevant departments.

    3. Key Discipline
    3.1 Biotechnology and Bioengineering - a national key discipline
    “Biotechnology and Bioengineering” is a biological product-oriented comprehensive discipline combining the principles and methods of biology, chemistry, physics and engineering. It aims to promote the healthy development of bioindustry via systematically design, optimization and engineering of biological systems and functions. The “Biotechnology and Bioengineering” discipline in our school is established following the constructions of national and provincial characteristic specialty of “Biotechnology and Bioengineering”.

    3.2 Food Science and Engineering
    Discipline of Food Science and Engineering as one of the first key constructing disciplines of Huaiyin Institute of Technology, was mainly center the production and storage technology of green agricultural, development and utilization of specialty food resources, food quality and testing technology, and food biotechnology.

    3.3 Crop science
    Crop science was selected as one of first major constructing discipline since 2013. It keeps the development strategy of staggering progress, serving region and forming peculiarity. It possesses a stable, reasonable team and four research fields with local specialties.
