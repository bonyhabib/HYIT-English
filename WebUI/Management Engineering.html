  • Faculty of Management Engineering

    • 1. Faculty Introduction
    The faculty offers four undergraduate programs in Information Management, Logistics Engineering, Industrial Engineering and Engineering Management, of which Logistics Engineering is the experimental program of Jiangsu Excellence Initiative. Based on the programs, the faculty has four teaching departments--Information Management Department, Logistics Engineering Department, Industrial Engineering Department and Engineering Management Department and one well-equipped experimental center. The faculty initiatively develops all kinds of cooperation with enterprises and governments, one of which is the joint teaching pattern-- off-campus praxis sites have been established the with over 40 enterprises.
    The faculty has an excellent well-structured teaching and research team of 36 staff members, including 30 full-time teachers. Among them, there are three professors, one school professor, and 11 associate professors. The senior professional title ratio reaches 39%. 14 with the PhD degree (including 3 doctoral students), accounting for 30.5% in all teachers.13 full-time teachers were selected for provincial and municipal training objects, including 1 for Jiangsu "333" Talent Project Third-level, 3 for Jiangsu "Blue Project" Middle-young Age Academic Leaders, 4 for Jiangsu "blue Project" Young Core Instructor, 5 for Huai’an "533" Talent Engineering.

    2. Undergraduate Programs Introduction
    2.1 Industrial Engineering
    Industrial Engineering has evolved as a main subject in engineering and management dealing with the development, improvement, and implementation of complex processes and systems by using the principles and methods of mathematical, physical, and social sciences. The effective utilization of industrial engineering has greatly contributed to our increased standard of living during the last century through increased productivity, quality of work and services, and improvements in working environments. The major aims to train specialized professional talents with the knowledge, qualities and abilities of modern industrial engineering, system management etc.

    2.2 Engineering Management
    The program is to train the application-oriented talents and innovative project management personnel with basic engineering sciences, humanities, management economics and other relevant knowledge and skills, skillful foreign language proficiency and computer skills, the skills of the construction of project demonstration and decision, investment control, project management ability in the whole process including tendering and bidding, certain engineering practice experience to meet the needs of socialist modernization. Based on the engineering science and engineering technical support and with the help of management and economics, the program fosters research-oriented, administrative, innovative, applied construction industry leaders and backup talents who are good at solving practical engineering problems and managing projects efficiently with excellent leadership skills and teamwork spirit.

    2.3 Information Management and System
    The program is to train the practice-oriented advanced professional personnel to meet the first-line working need in industry and regional economy society with modern management theory, computer science and technology, who grasp the knowledge and skills on systematic thought, information system analysis and design and information management with engineering technology background, strong practical ability, innovation and entrepreneurship and social responsibility.

    2.4 Logistics Engineering
    The program is mainly aimed to equip students with the basic theories and knowledge of logistics engineering, and to cultivate the first-line logistics engineer. It is to train the first-line applied senior engineering talents who can grasp the basic knowledge on logistics engineering technology, and are able to do logistics facilities analysis and planning, logistics technology application and development, logistics projects planning and operation. The talents can analyze, plan, design, operate and manage the modern logistics facilities, logistics system and supply chain in the modern logistics industry and business.
