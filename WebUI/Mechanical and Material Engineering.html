  • Faculty of Mechanical and Material Engineering

    • 1. Faculty Introduction
    FMME has 5 programs, including Mechanical Design Manufacture and Automation, Mechanical and Electronic Engineering, Material Forming and Control Engineering, Metal Material Engineering, and Process Equipment and Control Engineering, with Mechanical and Electronic Engineering being the first-level provincial key construction discipline of “Provincial Twelfth Five-year Plan”, Mechanical Design Manufacture and Automation being national featured specialty, national pilot specialty undertaking “A Plan for Educating and Training Outstanding Engineers (PETOE)”, and provincial excellent specialty as well as the provincial key discipline of “Provincial Twelfth Five-year Plan”; Material Forming and Control Engineering being provincial pilot specialty undertaking “PETOE” and provincial key discipline of “Provincial Twelfth Five-year Plan ”.
    FMME boast a staff of 82, among whom there are 13 professors, 12 postgraduate advisers, over 60 with doctor and master degree, 2 experts who enjoy special allowance from the State Council, 5 candidates for “Provincial Project 333” and “Qinglan Project”, and a provincial innovation team. In the past five years, the faculty members have finished more than 50 scientific research projects of national, provincial and municipal levels, among which there are 16 National Natural Science Foundation, over 60 patents approved in China, more than 20 software copyrights, and more than 10 awards of national and provincial levels. There are about 10 graduates and over 1800 undergraduates. In the past five years, more than 100 students have won prizes in various contests with significant impact, such as National Robot Contest, National Undergraduate Engineering Training Integration Ability Competition, 3DDS Contest, and Provincial Mechanics Competition for College Students. The employment rate maintains as high as 98 percent.

    2. Undergraduate Programs Introduction
    2.1 Mechanical Design & Manufacturing and Automation
    This program aims to train and cultivate kinds of high-quality engineering talents who have strong innovation capacity to meet the demands of economic and social development. It provides students with the general knowledge of this major such as the principles and methods of mechanical design, the principles and technologies of mechanical manufacturing engineering, the drive and control in mechanical system and basics for computer application, which enable students to acquire basic training of modern mechanical engineer and improve their basic ability of mechanical product design, manufacturing & equipment control and production organization management.

    2.2 Mechanical and Electronic Engineering
    This program aims to train and cultivate the top-level professional talents with professional basic knowledge and skills of mechanical and electronic engineering, who are qualified in such production-line work as the design and manufacturing, control development, applied research and production management of mechanical and electronic products. Graduates will master the comparatively broader basic theoretical knowledge on mechanical automation and the integration of mechanics and electronics, possess comparatively stronger practical engineering ability, and be qualified for such jobs as the systematic design, test, employment and maintenance of the mechanical and electronic system.

    2.3 Metal Material Engineering
    This program is one of the important pillars in national economic construction and plays a key role in various industries, such as aerospace industry, energy and chemical industry, national defense industry, metallurgical and mechanical electric. This major aims to cultivate the top-level engineering talents with knowledge of metal material science and engineering, who are qualified in doing research, developing new technology, doing process and facility design, production and management in the fields of metallurgical and material structure, formation of metal material and composite material as well as metal forming. This major has two directions, metal material specialty and casting and welding specialty.

    2.4 Material Forming & Control Engineering
    It is an inter-disciplinary major which integrate material science, forming process and automatic control technology and aims to train and cultivate engineers and technicians who have a good knowledge of basic theories of material forming and its application ability, and through modern engineers training, can be qualified in such work as the material forming, the processing techniques, the equipment design and development, scientific research, production management and marketing. This major has two directions, mould specialty and casting and welding specialty.
