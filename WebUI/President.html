    • Welcome to Huaiyin Institute of Technology (HYIT).


    Established in 1958, HYIT is a multi-disciplinary engineering Institute under Jiangsu Provincial Department of Education. It is located in Huaian, a renowned national historical and cultural city in the northern plain of Jiangsu Province. It is the birthplace of Zhou Enlai, the first Chinese Premier of P. R. C.


    HYIT has three campuses—Meicheng Road Campus, Beijing Road Campus and Xiaohu Campus—covering an area of about 2063 mu (about 146 hectares). HYIT consists of 19 faculties and colleges, offering bachelor or master degree in 64 programs. There are over 20,000 registered regular students and about 160 overseas students studying for bachelor degree or master degree. HYIT has over 1,200 faculty members, including 441 professors and associate professors, 902 with Doctor or master degrees, 158 postgraduate advisers, 55 enterprise advisers and 11 part-time advisers of Master and Doctorate candidates, 4 State Council Experts for special allowance, 1 provincial-level distinguished professor, and over 110“Top Scientists of Project 333” talents. HYIT actively promotes the academic internationalization and has been enhancing the linkage and cooperation with over 30 overseas universities in America, Canada, England, France, Japan, Australia, India, Singapore, Republic of Korea and so on.


    HYIT endeavors to provide the first class facilities and conditions for teaching, learning, experiment and research. For a long time, HYIT has been adhering to quality education and focusing on the cultivation of students’ creative and comprehensive abilities. Many of the graduates have become the backbones in every walk of life and made great contributions to the development of society.


    Whether you desire to receive high-quality education in mechanical engineering, to gain cutting-edge knowledge of computer science and engineering, or to pursue specialized academic training in business, life science and chemical engineering, transportation engineering, or the artistic design, the institute will offer you opportunities to grow and express leadership potential.


    We kindly invite you to join one of the Jiangsu’s fastest-growing institutions of higher learning, which is known for developing career-minded professionals who understand their own world and desire to serve as community leaders, engineers with strong practical skills and service-oriented professionals. And we would like you to be here and distinguish yourselves.
