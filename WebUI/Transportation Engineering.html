    • 1. Faculty Introduction
    Faculty of Transportation Engineering currently offers 5 undergraduate programs, namely, Vehicle Engineering, Automobile Service Engineering, Traffic Engineering, Transportation Engineering and Road Bridge & River-crossing Engineering, among which Mechanical Engineering (Vehicle Engineering) is the Provincial Key Construction Discipline, Vehicle Engineering and Transportation Engineering being the key disciplines in the Twelfth Five-year Plan of Jiangsu Province for Higher Education. Additionally, Vehicle Engineering and Automobile Service Engineering are the experimental programs in the plan, nationally and provincially, for Educating and Training Outstanding Engineers.
    At present, the faculty owns a Vehicle Engineering Laboratory, an Intelligent Transportation Laboratory and a Traffic Training Center, including 13 sub-laboratories of Vehicle Construction, Vehicle Electrical & Electronic Equipment, Vehicle Electronic Control System CAT, Automobile Detection & Diagnosis, Transportation Engineering, and Traffic Management & Control. They cover a built-up area of about 10,122 m2. It provides a favorable practical teaching environment with apparatus valued at more than 30 million RMB and 20 practice bases on and off campus.

    2. Undergraduate Programs Introduction
    2.1 Vehicle Engineering
    The program aims to cultivate application-oriented talents who obtain a basic training in vehicle engineering. Upon the completion of the program, students are supposed to own a solid theoretical knowledge basis and a superior ability in engineering practice, automobile design and manufacturing. They also develop a sense of innovation, entrepreneurial spirit, teamwork spirit and social responsibilities as well. They can engage in product development, manufacturing and other related work in the field of vehicle engineering.

    2.2 Automobile Service Engineering
    Based on the principle of all-round development in morality, intelligence, physique and art, the objective of this program is to cultivate professionals mastering solid theoretical automobile technology and service knowledge. Students will possess fundamental knowledge and skills in automobile marketing and service, maintenance and fault diagnosis. They will also be strengthened in practical automobile insurance and claims and be developed with the sense of innovation, entrepreneurship, cooperation and social responsibilities. They will be qualified to engage in marketing, after-sale service such as automobile maintenance and fault diagnosis, insurance and claims as practical talents meeting the demands of frontline work in automobile sales, technical service, application and management.

    2.3 Transportation
    Transportation professional training meets the needs of local economic and social development. Trained in an all-round manner in morality, intelligence and aestheticsmaster planning, students develop a sense of innovation and social responsibility. They master the professional skills in transportation planning, transportation organization, transportation management. They are capable of making transportation planning, transportation organization and management and can be engaged in the work of production plan, organization, command, decision making and rail transportation technical service in transportation field as advanced practical technicians.

    2.4 Traffic Engineering
    This program aims to equip students with basic theoretical knowledge of traffic engineering, and possess the primary abilities, which include planning and design for traffic engineering facilities, engineering construction for road traffic facilities, detection and maintenance for road traffic facilities. Transportation industry oriented, graduates who will be the applicable talents with high quality can engage in the field of planning, design, construction, management and operation.

    2.5 Road, Bridge and River-Crossing Engineering
    The program aims to cultivate advanced practical engineers and technicians with a basic training in transportation civil engineering. The graduates of this program should have the basic theories and skills of road and bridge design, construction, supervision and maintenance, construction management, innovative entrepreneurial spirit and team spirit and social responsibility sense for transportation and civil engineering industry. They can engage in roads, bridges and other civil engineering such as surveying, design, construction, project management, supervision, test and other related work.
