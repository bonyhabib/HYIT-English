    Message From President

    Welcome to Huaiyin Institute of Technology (HYIT). Established in 1958, HYIT is a multi-disciplinary engineering Institute under Jiangsu Provincial Department of Education. It is located in Huaian, a renowned national historical and cultural city in the northern plain of Jiangsu Province. It is the birthplace of Zhou Enlai, the first Chinese Premier of P. R. C.
